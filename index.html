Privacy Policy — LinkedInPowerMsg by ScouterZero

Last updated: March 10, 2026

1. Overview

LinkedInPowerMsg ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension developed by ScouterZero. This Privacy Policy explains what data the Extension collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. We are committed to a "Local-First" approach to privacy.

By installing and using the Extension, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect (Local-First Privacy)

The core functionality of LinkedInPowerMsg operates entirely on your device. The following data is never transmitted to any external server during normal use:

  • LinkedIn Content: Your message content, profile information, connections, or activity.
  • User Assets: Your saved message snippets, labels, shortcut keys, and file attachments.
  • Browsing History: We do not track your activity on any website outside of LinkedIn.

All snippets and attachments are stored locally using chrome.storage.local and never leave your browser.

3. Data We Collect — Waitlist Sign-Up (Optional)

To help us transition to a sustainable model, the Extension includes a usage limit. When you voluntarily join the early-access waitlist (displayed after reaching the usage limit), the following information is collected:

Data Point Purpose
Email Address To notify you regarding the launch of our paid plans and product updates.
Feedback Text Optional; used to improve product features and user experience.
Submission Metadata Timestamp and consent flag to manage waitlist order and compliance.

Data Transmission & Disclosure

When you explicitly click "Join Waitlist," your data is sent via an HTTPS POST request to a Google Apps Script endpoint. This data is stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to the ScouterZero development team.

Note: The Extension will never collect your email address automatically. It requires your active input and submission.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Apps Script (Google LLC) to process waitlist submissions. No other third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking tools are used.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Data Type Location Retention Period
Local Extension Data chrome.storage.local Until uninstalled or manually cleared.
Waitlist Information Google Sheets (Private) Until product launch or upon deletion request.

6. Permissions Justification

In compliance with the Chrome Web Store's "Minimum Permission" policy, we use:

  • storage & unlimitedStorage: To save your templates and large base64-encoded attachments locally on your machine.
  • host_permissions (script.google.com): Specifically to allow the optional waitlist form to reach our database.
  • linkedin.com: The content script runs only on LinkedIn to provide messaging shortcuts.

7. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access or delete any personal information we hold.

  • To delete local data: Simply uninstall the extension or clear your browser storage.
  • To delete waitlist data: If you provided your email and wish to be removed from our records, please contact us.
